# ✨ Yay or Nay ✨

A sleek and modern web application where visitors can rate attractive people by voting "Yay" or "Nay". The app keeps track of all ratings and allows users to review their choices.

## Features

✨ **Beautiful & Sexy Design**
- Modern gradient backgrounds with animated stars
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Responsive design that works on all devices
- Glass-morphism effects with backdrop filters

👍 **Rating System**
- Vote "Yay" or "Nay" on displayed images
- Real-time counter updates
- Smooth button animations and feedback

📊 **Statistics Dashboard**
- Live counter for Yays and Nays
- Elegant stat cards with hover effects
- Visual representation of ratings

📸 **Results Gallery**
- View all Yay and Nay rated images
- Click images to enlarge and view in detail
- Organized grid layout
- Persistent storage using browser's LocalStorage

💾 **Data Persistence**
- All ratings are saved to your browser's LocalStorage
- Your votes are preserved even after closing the app
- Easy to clear data by resetting browser storage

## How to Use

1. **Open the webpage** - Navigate to `index.html` in your browser
2. **View a person** - A random person's image will be displayed
3. **Make your vote** - Click either the "Yay" or "Nay" button
4. **See the results** - The counters update immediately
5. **View your history** - Click "See All Yays" or "See All Nays" to see all your votes
6. **Get the next person** - Click "Next Person" to see another image
